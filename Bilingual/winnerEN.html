Sending the sentences


Because most of the people that took the time to read and understand our ideas and strategies are the kind of people we want to have in this community we figured out maybe you would also like to do one of these two things. (Or maybe both, who knows?!)


1. Bookmark this site so you can come back to it when you're ready to send us your sentences. (Here’s the template)


2. Share our idea with at least 3 persons you think may find something that benefits them.



Don't forget to add in the mail:


  • Your Facebook name (So we can add you to the community)
  • The excel document (Specifying if any of the languages is not perfect grammatically)
  • And if you feel like, share with us some thoughts or suggestions. We love hearing from the future members of the community.

If the button "Upload notes!" does not work, just send us a mail with your sentences to...

worldwideslang.contact@gmail.com
