Writing down the sentences


In the next few weeks you'll be writing down words or sentences that come to your mind as “Useful”, and then you’ll translate them from your mother language to your second language. (If you want to take it even further you can also add translations to a third language in case you have one)


You're responsible of managing your time however you want, but since the strategies used in the challenge 30 Days without (Language you "can't" stop speaking) that we're currently encouraging exchange students to do may benefit you somehow we thought it would be a good idea to share it also with you.


30 Days without (Language you "can't" stop speaking)


Through this strategy you'll focus on the goal you want to accomplish (Whether is writing 10 or maybe 20 sentences a day) plus you'll learn through experience the importance of creating a support group.


Hopefully this strategy will not disappear from your life after you accomplish the challenge.


Important


You can decide to write the sentences down immediately on an excel spreadsheet like this one since the beginning, but that's not what we recommend you to do. When it comes to language learning strategies nothing is better than a piece of paper and a pencil.
