Introduction


Hi there, I'm László Oltványi Co-Founder of World Wide Slang, I just wanted to thank you for spending a little time with us. I'll be explaining you how this community works and also how you can become a part of it.


As you may already know our goal is to create what we like to call "The World Wide Slang". Currently we have exchange students making an effort to grab the most useful sentences in an exchange environment, and we understand people without the freedoms of an exchange student do not have enough time to do an intensive challenge. So that's when we came up with this idea...


World Wide Slang offers you the opportunity to join the people that have made the same effort as you. The size of your contribution will determine the amount of information you’ll have access to. The first group consists of people that have made a contribution of at least 50, the second one 150 and the third one 300 useful words or sentences that are used in everyday life.


So in a few words if you contribute with 50 words or sentences you’ll have access to the first group containing everyone’s first 50 phrases. If after that you send 100 more you’ll enter to the 2nd group and last but not least if after that you send 150 extra you will not only have access to the 3rd group in which all the sentences from the previous groups will be uploaded but you’ll also have the opportunity to become a partner. (More info on becoming a partner here)



If you want to start right away sharing with the world those strange expressions, useful words and sentences then click the "Next step" button.
