Partner


What are the benefits of being a partner?


  • Being able to say you're officially part of this movement (That can be added on a resume)
  • Meet and interact with partners around the world
  • Participate in the decision making process
  • Obtain the latest information on how the project is going

What do I have to do to become a partner?


  • Speak english
  • You must successfully upload 300 words/sentences
  • Successfully convince 10 persons to join the community
  • Make creative contributions to enhance the user's experience
  • Participate on online video hangouts when necessary

If you're interested in becoming a partner you'll find more information about it on the third group after contributing with at least 300 words/sentences.
